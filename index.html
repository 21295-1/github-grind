Best Composers in Film


Composers are musicians who write(or compose) music. Composers are extremely common in the music industry, but they are also vital to the film industry.
Many iconic films would not be the same without composers. Be it legendary westerns like the Good, The Bad, and the Ugly, or iconic horror slashers like Halloween, film composers are capable of elevating the quality of a film the next level.
This list is dedicated to some of the best film composers of all time.






01

ENNIO MORRICONE


Ennio Morricone may be the greated film composer of all time.
His most known work is on Westerns, particularly by the director Sergio Leone. His score for the "Good the Bad and The Ugly is iconic", and has been refrenced and reused by countless other films. He usually uses orchestra's to give his music a truely epic feel. He is also incredibly versatile, creating a brilliant synth ambient score for Carpenter's horror masterpiece "The Thing".


02

JOHN WILLIAMS


John Williams is another legend in the field of film scores. He is known for his frequent collobarations with Steven Spielberg, most notably his works on Jaws and Jurassic Park.


03

HANS ZIMMER


Hans Zimmer is the greatest film composer alive right now. His works on Interstellar and many other Christopher Nolan films are some of the most popular and brilliant film scores ever made.
